International Women’s Day 2021

International Women’s Day (IWD), is celebrated on 8th March every year. This year, Aarogya Mela was organized for community women for the early detection and management of common health issues faced by women such as Bone calcium deficiency, Anaemia and Thyroid issues.

In this Aarogya Mela, Niramaya conducted:

  • Thyroid Screening Camp
  • Bone Density camp
  • Anaemia Detection camp
  • Blood sugar Testing Camp
  • Breast and cervical cancer screening Camp

1003 community women visited and benefitted through this “Aarogya Mela”.
