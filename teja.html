Project TEJA- Adolescent Anemia Detection and Reduction Program

The project TEJA is being implemented at Chembur and Govandi slums with the support of Sterling and Wilson Private Limited. The project aims to reduce the incidence of Iron deficiency Anemia among Adolescent girls and improve their dietary practices. 3 thousand adolescent girls along with their families have been covered under this program since year 2019-20. 85% improvement in the hemoglobin levels of identified anemic girls have been achieved under this project.
