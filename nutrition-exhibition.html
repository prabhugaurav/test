Nutrition Exhibition

Nutrition Exhibition was organized at Garib Janta Nagar and Shivshakti Nagar Community from Colaba Backbay in the month of February 2021. The objectives of the nutritional Exhibition program was to create awareness aboutimportance of nutritional dietary practices and common nutritional deficiencies detected in the community.

A colourful IEC exhibits on various aspects of good and healthy nutrition were displayed during this event. There were various posters depicting important messages on nutrition, prevention of vitamin deficiency disorders, IYCF (Infant and young children feeding practices) were displayed in the exhibition.

430 community people visited this exhibition.
