Our Story

ॐ सर्वे भवन्तु सुखिनः । सर्वे सन्तु निरामयाः ।
सर्वे भद्राणि पश्यन्तु । मा कश्चित् दुःख भाग्भवेत् ॥
May all be happy, May all be free from illness,
May all see what is auspicious, May no one suffer.
— We believes creating community healthy

Niramaya Health Foundation believes in this Sanskrit Phrase and work towards creating the community which is healthy, well aware and wise to take appropriate life decisions.

Niramaya Health Foundation is Not for Profit Organization registered under Section 8 companies act, working in Mumbai slums and rural parts of India for past 20 years. Niramaya works on three important components of the population i.e. Children, Mothers and Adolescents to break intergenerational cycle of the malnutrition. Along with its Health and nutrition program, the organization also tries to address related social causes such as addiction, Gender issues which affect individual’s health and nutrition.


— Our model of change

Niramaya Health Foundation works with urban poor to enable them to practice healthy dietary and hygiene practices and avail available government health services through awareness creation and better linkages with local government agencies.

Tackling nutritional issues of urban slums is complex, Niramaya adapts multi-dimensional approach where we work with individuals, communities and government to address this issue in holistic manner.
